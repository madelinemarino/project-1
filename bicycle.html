DAKOTA

June 4-10, 2017

Start in Sioux Falls at Falls Park

RASDak 2016 was another great year of cyclists from all over the country riding across South Dakota. We are a nonprofit organization that works with each overnight community to provide a safe riding experience for the riders while educating the communities along the route about cycling and cycling safety.

Mark your calendar to come join us next year, starting Sunday, June 4, 2017! More details will be revealed this Fall as the 5th annual Ride Across South Dakota takes place June 2017
